# B.R.O.Y.L.E.S. HUB

**Building Rights Over Your Life — Ensuring Safety**

The official online platform and digital home for the B.R.O.Y.L.E.S. movement.

## In Memory of Robert Anthony “Tony” Broyles Jr.

Tony was murdered inside Eastern Kentucky Correctional Complex (EKCC) on August 31, 2025 — just **nine days** before his scheduled release. This site was built by his family with love, grief, and unwavering determination.

---

## Mission

B.R.O.Y.L.E.S. HUB exists so no family has to fight alone against bureaucracy, redactions, and cover-ups after losing a loved one in Kentucky’s prison system.

We turn grief into organized action — demanding truth, transparency, and systemic reform.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Tony’s Story** — His life, transformation, and final days
- **Report an Incident** — Secure, trauma-informed reporting tool for families
- **Facilities Directory** — Every Kentucky correctional institution
- **Policy Library** — KDOC policies, open records guides, and legal templates
- **Resources** — Grief support, legal aid, victim compensation, and more
- **News & Updates** — Developments in Tony’s case and broader reform efforts
- **Accountability Dashboard** — Trends and verified incidents
- **Memorials & Stories** — Space to honor loved ones
